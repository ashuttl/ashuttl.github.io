What is RSS?

RSS stands for “really simple syndication” (seriously). It’s a technology that makes it easy to keep track of updates to your favorite websites.

An RSS feed is a special kind of web page that’s designed to be read by computers rather than people. It provides real-time notification of changes to particular site – not unlike a modern version of the old ticker-tape news wire machines.

Our RSS feed provides updates from our blog.
