How to buy tickets  

PCA Great Performances tickets are available exclusively through PortTix, the box office at Merrill Auditorium. Tickets may be purchased online, by telephone, or in person.

At this time, only full-price adult tickets may be purchased online. If you qualify for any subscriber, student, senior, or promotional discount, please contact the box office directly to purchase your tickets.

subscribe to PCA

Anybody can become a PCA Great Performances subscriber. Simply purchase tickets to at least four events in our subscription season (in one transaction). As a subscriber, you’ll get:

  • Discounts of up to 10% for all performances in our subscription season.
  • Advance notice of our season and special events, as well as first dibs on tickets.
  • Subscriber discounts on additional tickets purchased any time during the season.
  • Exchange privileges, should a date prove to be inconvenient, up to twenty-four hours before the show.

To subscribe, please call a box office sales agent at 207.842.0800 or download and submit this form.

discounted tickets available

  • Groups of ten or more (twenty for some performances) are eligible for discounted tickets. Read more about group services.
  • Audience members over 65 receive a 10% discount.
  • Children under 18 and full-time students can receive deeply discounted tickets to most of our shows. These discounted prices, where available, are listed on this website.

Discounted tickets may be subject to limited availability.
