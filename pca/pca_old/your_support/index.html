individual contributions

As a nonprofit organization and a community presence, we rely on the generosity of friends, businesses and foundations for the critical annual support needed to sustain our performance, education and outreach activities.

Each season we create and administer educational activities to accompany many of our mainstage events. Ticket revenue does not support our education program; we rely on donations from those who believe that the performing arts contribute not only to the quality of life in Portland today, but also to the development of the generations who will be Portland tomorrow. We believe that opening ears and minds to the rewards of the performing arts is part of our job.

Please help by contributing to our 2007–08 Annual Fund. We need and would truly appreciate your help.

Please print and complete this form to accompany your gift. Send your contribution to:

PCA Great Performances

PCA Great Performances is a 501(c)3 organization, and gifts are tax deductible as allowed by law.
