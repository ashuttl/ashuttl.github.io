Directions to Merrill Auditorium  

To make it easier to get to and park at Merrill Auditorium, we've put together this interactive Google map of downtown Portland. Merrill Auditorium is represented by the pushpin; some nearby public parking lots and garages are represented by the blue Ps. Click the pushpin or Ps to generate directions from your location.


view larger map

If this map doesn’t work for you, or if you still need help, please or call the box office for assistance at 207.842.0800.

textual directions

For those prefer text directions, we’ve put together some of those, too. Merrill Auditorium is adjacent to City Hall in downtown Portland, Maine. Its street address is 20 Myrtle Street, Portland ME 04101.

From Northern Maine

Take Interstate 295 into Portland. Use exit 7 from 295 onto Franklin Arterial. Continue on Franklin Arterial until you intersect with Congress Street. Take a right onto Congress Street. Continue straight through the traffic light at Pearl Street and take your next right onto Myrtle Street, just before City Hall. The main lobby entrance to Merrill Auditorium is about 1/4 the way down Myrtle Street, on the left side of the street. The PortTix Box Office is located just inside the main entrance.

From Massachusetts, New Hampshire and Southern Maine

Take Interstate 95 into Maine. Use exit 44 from 95 onto Interstate 295. Use exit 7 from 295 onto Franklin Arterial. Continue on Franklin Arterial until you intersect with Congress Street. Take a right onto Congress Street. Continue straight through the traffic light at Pearl Street and take your next right onto Myrtle Street, just before City Hall. The main lobby entrance to Merrill Auditorium is about 1/4 the way down Myrtle Street, on the left side of the street. The PortTix Box Office is located just inside the main entrance.

From Northern New Hampshire and Western Maine

Take Route 302 into Portland. Once in Portland, Route 302 becomes Forest Avenue. Continue on Forest Avenue until it ends at the intersection with Congress Street. Turn left onto Congress Street and continue until you see City Hall on the left. Turn left onto Myrtle Street immediately after City Hall. The main lobby entrance to Merrill Auditorium is about 1/4 the way down Myrtle Street, on the left side of the street. The PortTix Box Office is located in the main lobby.
