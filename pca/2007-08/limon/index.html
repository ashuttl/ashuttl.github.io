Limón Dance Company  

buy tickets

Wednesday, February 27, 2008   7:30 pm
free lecture: 6 pm
Merrill Auditorium, Portland, Maine

Acclaimed for its dramatic expression, technical mastery and expansive, nuanced movement, the Limón Dance Company illustrates the timelessness of its founder, José Limón. Mr. Limón, one of the true masters of modern dance, is placed firmly in the legacy of contemporary dance shared by Martha Graham, Isadora Duncan, Alvin Ailey and others. Now celebrating its sixtieth anniversary, his company is more than ever an example of why American dance took the world by storm, expressing through dazzing theatrical movement the turns and celebrations of the human spirit. The company performs reconstructed works as part of the National Endowment for the Arts “American Masters: Dance” program.

PCA Offstage presents…

Masterclass with Limón Dance Company

Tuesday, February 26, 2008   7–8:30 pm
Portland Arts and Technology High School (PATHS)
$20 class fee   $10 audit fee if space allows

Rich in dynamics, expression and musicality, the Limón Dance Company celebrates sixty years. Learn the technique and repertory of the iconic, classic contemporary dance master José Limón from a company member. For intermediate dancers.

Limón: A Life Beyond Words

Wednesday, February 27, 2008   6–7 pm
Merrill Auditorium Rehearsal Hall
FREE

Learn about the celebrated dance maker, José Limón, through this award-winning and poetic documentary that offers a compelling look at the life and work of this revolutionary artist. Portland Ballet Company’s Vanessa Beyland will introduce Limón: A Life Beyond Words. Ms. Beyland is a graduate of NYU’s Tisch School of the Arts with a BFA in Dance. Prior to coming to Maine, she worked for the Limón Dance Company in New York.

“For drama, virtuosity and grace, there is no finer company.” Village Voice
