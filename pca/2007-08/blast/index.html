The Tony– and Emmy-winning theatrical sensation Blast! commands the stage with forty-eight brass, percussion, and movement artists brought together in a unique explosion of music and theater. Blast! is a stomping, non-stop mix of kaleidescopic spectacle, rousing sound, and whirling showmanship, promising to wow audiences of all ages.
