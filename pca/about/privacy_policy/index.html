Privacy policy  

PCA Great Performances is committed to safeguarding your privacy. This document describes the types of information we collect about you and what we do with it. Last revised January 13, 2008.

how we collect information

We collect information you provide about yourself when you:

  • purchase PCA Great Performances tickets from PortTix
  • contact our offices to request materials or information
  • register on our website to receive our email newsletters

From time to time, in addition to information our patrons provide to us, we receive lists of names and postal addresses from Portland-area nonprofits. These lists are used for one-time mailings, usually of our annual brochure, and are destroyed after use.

We never purchase lists. We also never purchase, seek out, or accept lists of email addresses or phone numbers.

how we use information

Email Addresses

If you provide us or PortTix with your email address, we may send you our periodic email newsletter, in addition to occasional “postcard” emails when we have something special to announce.

We only want to email you if you want to hear from us. You have the right to opt out of these emails at any time by contacting our offices or by clicking the “unsubscribe” link at the bottom of any marketing email.

We send our email newsletters using an internet mailing service called MailChimp. MailChimp will never use your information except on our behalf. If you have concerns, we encourage you to read their terms of service.

We never share our list of email addresses with any other organizations.

Postal Addresses

If you supply us or PortTix with your postal address you may receive mailings from us with information on your purchases or future events. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please let us know by contacting our offices.

We may periodically share your name and postal address with other local nonprofit organizations for one-time-only use. If you wish, you may have your information put on our do-not-share list by writing to us at the above address. Please provide us with your exact name and address and we will do our best to ensure your name is removed from any lists we share with other organizations.

Phone Numbers

Your phone number will only be used to contact you about any problems related to your ticket purchases and to directly respond to you when you have contacted us. We do not telemarket and we do not share phone numbers with any other organizations.

privacy on the website

Cookies

You may find that our website has saved cookies to your computer. These are a function of the software that powers our website. These cookies cannot be used to personally identify you and they are not used to track your movements.

Tracking

From time to time, PCA Great Performances may use various third-party services – such as Google Analytics, CrazyEgg, Clicky, RobotReplay, and ClickTale – to track visitors to our website. We use these services to collect aggregate data on the technical capabilities of visitors’ computers, on the number and timing of visits to our web pages, and on visitors’ movements through our site. No personally identifying information is ever tracked or stored. The data we gather are used internally to better understand and support our users.

important notes

We reserve the right make changes to this policy at any time. Changes to this policy will be posted online at this location.

This policy does not apply to our box office, PortTix. For information on how PortTix uses patron data, please view the PortTix privacy policy.
